Protea Hotels by Marriott® is a highly esteemed hospitality brand renowned for its extensive footprint across Africa. With hotels in South Africa, Zambia, Nigeria, Botswana, Namibia, Ghana, Tanzania, Uganda, Angola, and Malawi, we have the largest strategic presence on the continent. The Protea Hotels by Marriott portfolio comprises two brands: the mid-upmarket Protea Hotels by Marriott, including the lifestyle brand Protea Hotel Fire & Ice! by Marriott. The portfolio presents a full and diverse range of outstanding hotels, each is uniquely designed with its own personality, providing guests an opportunity to get a taste of the local flavor in a truly authentic way. Protea Hotels by Marriott is proud to be part of Marriott International, the world’s largest hotel company based in Bethesda, Maryland, USA, with 8500+ properties across 138 territories, including gateway cities such as London, Dubai and New York.
